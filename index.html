Mission

This project aims to enhance the user experience on the Three Rivers Trail in Humboldt County, Iowa through a cohesive, user friendly, and well-designed wayfinding signage system.

Vision

A unified trail experience linking ecosystems, cultures, and people.

Features

Eco-friendly materials, rest stops, historical signage, and scenic routes.

Volunteer, donate, or share your story with the trail community.
